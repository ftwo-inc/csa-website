Our promises to you:

About us

‘To make Career information accessible and actionable for people, education provider business and local authorities.’

Digital Profile is free to use (forever for individuals), with subscriptions for advanced features (refer to Subscriber Agreement). The high one-off fees used by recruiters encourage poor practice. Through transparent and fair pricing and a model that promotes high-quality matching, we want to make it faster, smoother and easier to find the right opportunity for you.

Ethics

We strive to build privacy respectful services that helps you create the career you want now and in the future - central element within our mission. We want to make everything transparent and usable.

We want to achieve our mission in the most Ethical way possible and challenge current industry approaches, such as how the Data (CV) is retained/used as people are contacted months or even years later.

How do we go about this

  1. All business have to be approved on our platform, this is to ensure that no recruitment agencies are able to use this, even if they wanted to pay.
  2. An individual can share their profile (data) with anyone by creating a share link, which can then be shared. You can then remove this link…
  1. We won't use any of your data for marketing (unless you have consented) or sell to a third party.
  2. We set your profile as private when you first set it up, controlling the status of your profile (CV)- You can change this at any time.
    1. Private - No one can see this, you don't appear in any Talent pool or search
    2. Public - You are open on the platform and internet (check)
    3. Incognito - You appear in searches but personal details name, location, pictures are removed or replaced with a default one. Employers then need to request access to see your, you can approve and reject this.

Data Story

As soon as you arrive on Digital Profile we are collecting data, some of which you can opt-out of and some which you can't (see cookie policy).

As a company, we make every effort to use your data and relevant analytics to improve how information is presented. Below are examples of how we will use your data.

We want to make developing a Career or finding the best Talent easier. Finding a new job is time-consuming and can be deflating. If you are happy in your role/career and just want some development, this can also be challenging. Our aim is to automate most of this work - Making it better for you. We do this by profiling your data.

Legal basis for this data usage, contract

Marketing purposes (with your consent)

Sending you emails about new features, products, services, and content.

Legal basis for this data usage: contract

Here is what each of these "legal bases" mean:

Consent

You have given clear consent for you to process your personal data for a specific purpose.

You can change your mind!

If you have previously given consent to our processing your data you can freely withdraw such consent at any time. You can remove this through your setting.

If you do withdraw your consent, and if we do not have another legal basis for processing your information, then we will stop processing your personal data. If we do have another legal basis for processing your information, then we may continue to do so subject to your legal rights.

Contract

Processing your data is necessary for a contract you have with us and to provide the service we have offered/agreed to.
